The Race to High Technology

The High Technology inudstry has been growing and countries that invest in this field have had amazing returns. Many countires have made high-tech products their main exports.

High-technology exports are products with high R&D intensity, such as in aerospace, computers, pharmaceuticals, scientific instruments, and electrical machinery. The goal of my data visualiztion project is to compare the High-Technology industry with total exports and total GDPs of several export oriented countries.
